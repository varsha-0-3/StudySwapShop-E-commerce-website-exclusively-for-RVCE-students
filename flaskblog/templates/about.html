About Us

Welcome to SSS (Study, Swap, Shop), An E-Commerce platform exclusively handmade for RVCE students! At SSS, we bring together a vibrant community of learners across the campus, providing a unique marketplace for old students and peers. Here we allow Ex-RVCEians and current students to Buy/Sell/Borrow various Experiential Learning Materials, Textbooks and Miscellaneous resources aimed for productive usage of resources. Elevate your learning experience here at SSS, where academic success and profit is just a click away...



Our Services


Sell

Users can sell various products by posting with image and attaching a resonable cost to it.

Buy

Users can buy various products by clicking on the relevant posts and viewing its details. For any product related queries one can contact owner directly through mail.

Borrow

Users can borrow various products for certain amount of time either by renting or freely depending upon the seller.
